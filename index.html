About

Ghost is a Swedish rock band that was formed in Link√∂ping in 2006. Their genre is specific to punk rock and heavy metal.

Ghost is known for its eccentric on-stage presence. Lead Singer, Tobias Forge, would go by "Papa Emeritus", dressed in Papal regalia and his face painted to resemble a skull. Seven of the group's eight members, known as "Nameless Ghouls", wear virtually identical, face-concealing costumes.

They have been active since 2006 and have released six studio albums and five live albums. Ghost has been nominated for seven Grammy Awards, and has won five. They have also been nominated for other less known awards and won those awards.
